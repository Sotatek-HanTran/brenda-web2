The destination bucket is where the workers will place the completed frames.

  1. Log in to AWS and click on S3
  2. If a destination bucket does not already exist, click the Create Bucket button and follow the prompts to create one
  3. Once the bucket is created, populate the "Frame Destination Bucket" field with the bucket in the format:
    s3://BUCKET_NAME
    Where BUCKET_NAME is the name of the destination bucket
